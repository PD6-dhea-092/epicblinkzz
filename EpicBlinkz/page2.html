ALL ABOUT MEMBER BLACKPINK

LISA (Lalisa Manobal) :

In 2010, at age 13, Lisa auditioned to join South Korean record label YG Entertainment in Thailand. She gained an interest in the K-pop industry in her childhood, admiring artists such as BigBang and 2NE1, and aspired to follow a similar path. Among the 4,000 applicants, she was the only individual to qualify. In 2011, Lisa moved to South Korea to begin her formal training as a trainee. She officially joined the label as its first non-ethnically Korean trainee on April 11. In November 2013, she appeared in the music video for labelmate Taeyang's single "Ringa Linga" as a background dancer alongside members of labelmates and boy bands iKon and Winner. In March 2015, Lisa undertook her first modelling job for the streetwear brand Nona9on, followed by South Korean cosmetics brand Moonshot in 2016. In August 2016, Lisa debuted as one of four members of girl group BLACKPINK, the first non-ethnically Korean artist under the label.

JISOO :

In 2011, Jisoo joined YG Entertainment as a trainee after passing its audition. Before debut, Jisoo appeared in various commercial advertisements (CF), such as Samsonite RED with actor Lee Min-ho and Smart Uniform with their seniors iKON. October 2014, she appeared in Epik High's music video and Hi Suhyun's music video. In 2015, Jisoo made a cameo appearance in the KBS2 drama The Producers with labelmates Sandara Park of 2NE1 and Kang Seung-yoon of Winner. On August 8 2016, Jisoo debuted as one of the four members of BLACKPINK

JENNIE :

When she was 14 years old, Jennie entered and joined YG Entertainment in 2010 as a trainee. In 2012, Jennie was referred to as the "Mystery Girl" when YG Entertainment was rumored to be forming a new girl group. Since 2012, Jennie has also modeled several times and collaborated with YG Entertainment artists. On 1st June 2016 , Jennie was the first member to be revealed for YG Entertainment's newest girl group, its first in seven years since 2NE1. On August 8, 2016, she debuted as a member of BLACKPINK.

ROSE :

In 2012, Rosé attended an audition in Sydney, Australia for YG Entertainment at her father's suggestion, she finished first among the 700 participant Two months later, she had signed with the label as a trainee, and moved to Seoul, South Korea. The same year, Rosé was given an opportunity to feature on labelmate G-Dragon's song "Without You" from his extended play (EP) One of a Kind (2012). Her name was not publicised at the time of release, her credit was revealed only following her announcement as a member of Blackpink. Rosé trained at YG Entertainment for four years before she was revealed as the final member of the girl group BLACKPINK on 22 June 2016.
